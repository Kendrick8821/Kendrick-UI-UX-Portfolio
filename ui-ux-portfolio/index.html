RENTAL
APP

MOBILE DESIGN

ABOUT THIS APPLICATION

RentGear is a peer-to-peer app that lets people rent out or borrow creative equipment from cameras and lighting to laptops and design tools. It connects local owners with renters who need gear for projects, events, or trials without the cost of buying.

Users browse by category (Camera, Videography, Audio, etc.), filter by location/price, and book securely in app. Owners list their items, set availability, and earn extra income. All through a simple, trusted interface.

Designed for speed and clarity, RentGear reduces friction in rentals with verified profiles, instant booking, and clear terms. It turns gear into opportunity and helps creators get what they need, fast.

PROBLEM STATEMENT

Young professionals often need high-quality video equipment to produce crucial work content, but they struggle to afford purchasing these expensive tools outright. This financial barrier limits their ability to create professional-grade videos, hindering their career growth.

SOLUTION

RentGear is a peer-to-peer equipment rental platform that connects young professionals and students with local owners of cameras, lightings, audios, and other creative tools. Instead of buying expensive gear, users can rent it affordably for short-term projects all through a simple, trusted mobile app.

GOALS

To create a trusted peer-to-peer marketplace that bridges the gap between equipment owners and young professionals, making high-end filming gear accessible without the high cost of ownership.

TARGET USERS

1 Young professional & freelancers
2 Professional photographers with limited budget
3 High school student for their personal project
4 Small event organizers
5 Tech enthusiasts & Early adopters

UX Decision

1

Claim Voucher Button

Challenge

High equipment costs create a barrier for students and freelancers with limited budgets. They often hesitate to book without financial incentives or discounts.

Decision

Introduced a prominent 'Claim Voucher' banner on the homepage offering up to 20% off for new users.

Why

This lowers the entry barrier, making professional gear more accessible for budget-conscious users. It incentivizes the first booking and encourages retention by providing immediate value.

2

Category

Challenge

Users often browse exploratorily and get overwhelmed by long text-heavy lists. They need to find specific equipment types (e.g., Lighting vs. Camera) quickly without reading every item.

Decision

Designed a visual grid system with distinct icons for each category and a 'See All' link for overflow content.

Why

Visual icons are processed faster by the brain than text. This reduces cognitive load, allowing users to scan and navigate to their needed gear instantly, making the browsing experience feel effortless and intuitive.
